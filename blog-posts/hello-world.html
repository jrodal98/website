by Jacob RodalDecember 9th, 2019

Like many of my peers, I do not enjoy studying for exams, especially computer science exams -  I would much rather spend my time building software instead.  Unfortunately for me, an uncontrollable urge to completely redesign my website struck at an inconvenient time, but hey, there are worse ways I could have procrastinated.  I didn't change much other than splitting my content across multiple pages (which could be seen as a downgrade), but one thing I didn't have before is a blog.

Why I added a blog to my website:

I've worked on quite a few personal projects over the last two years, most of which can be found on my github.  Some of them, however, don't nessarily belong there, such as my guide on how to launch scripts on a linux computer using with a smart watch.  Some projects, such as my ongoing efforts to build a r/unixporn worthy desktop, could be documented here instead of github (well, technically this website is hosted on github's servers, so...).
